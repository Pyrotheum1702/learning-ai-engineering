# My Trip to Japan

This is a short story about my trip. It was **amazing** and a little *tiring*.

## Places I visited

I spent most of my time in the city, but also took a few day trips.

- Tokyo
- Kyoto
- Osaka

## Food I ate

The food was the **best part**. Some dishes were *very* spicy.

- ramen
- sushi
- takoyaki

### Final thoughts

I would *definitely* go again. It was a **once-in-a-lifetime** trip.
