# Roles and Responsibilities

> _Question to answer in my own words: What are the roles and responsibilities of an AI Engineer?_

## Answer

AI Engineers are responsible for designing, developing, deploying AI systems that solve real-world problems.

Their roles include building machine learning models, implementing data processing pipeline, integrating AI solutions into existing software and platforms.

They work on tasks like data collection, cleaning, and labeling, as well as model training, testing, and optimization to ensure high performance and accuracy.

AI Engineers also focus on scaling models for production use, monitoring their performance, and troubleshooting issues.

Additionally, they collaborate with data scientists, software developers, and other stakeholders to align AI projects with business goals, ensuring that solutions are reliable, efficient, and ethically sound.
